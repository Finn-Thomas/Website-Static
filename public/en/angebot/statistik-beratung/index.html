Individual Coaching

Individual coaching in academic writing is a guided, process-oriented form of support that helps you refine your research question, line of argument, and text structure, plan your writing process, and develop your manuscript purposefully through to submission or final delivery.

starHighlights
  • checkClarifying the research question, objectives, and central narrative thread
  • checkBuilding structure and argumentation (outline, chapter/section logic)
  • checkFeedback on drafts: clarity, style, coherence (including strategies for overcoming writer’s block)
task_altDeliverables
  • checkPersonalized writing and timeline plan (milestones, routines, priorities)
  • checkAnnotated feedback on drafts with concrete, actionable revisions
  • checkOptional: templates/checklists and a final review before submission/delivery

Academic Editing

Scientific editing typically includes language and style polishing (grammar, academic tone, consistent terminology) as well as improving structure and argumentation (clear logic, coherence, a strong narrative thread). It often also covers consistency checks for formal elements such as citations, the reference list, and the correct integration of tables and figures.

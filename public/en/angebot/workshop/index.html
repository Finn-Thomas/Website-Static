What is it about?

A compact 1-day workshop that covers the essential basics for a successful thesis and applies them directly to your topic—from a clear research question and a solid outline to methodology, citation logic, academic style, and workflow organization. By the end of the day, you will have clear next steps and a realistic plan to enter the writing phase with confidence.
